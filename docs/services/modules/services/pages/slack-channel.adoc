= Slack Channel
:page-platform: Premium Support, Advisory Services
:page-description: Slack Channel

A Slack channel, hosted by CircleCI, provides faster response to technical questions, or service issues, and a direct line to your CircleCI account team. 

== Benefits

Teams that join a Slack channel with their account team:

* Have a faster feedback loop for product feedback and technical questions.
* Gain access to information on upcoming features and feature previews.
* Are able to easily schedule touch points, both commercial and technical.
* Have a central place for CircleCI questions and information.

== Intended Audience

The channel is intended to facilitate communication with your CircleCI account team. Anyone interacting with CircleCI at your organization is welcome.

NOTE: The channel is not intended to replace the Support ticketing system for break/fix issues.
